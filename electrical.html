Our Specialty

Electrical Services
We Provide

From new construction to small upgrades — our master electricians handle it all safely and to code, in your home or business.

🏠

New Home Electrical

Building a new home? We handle the complete electrical installation from start to finish — service panels, circuit breakers, all wiring, outlets, switches, and light fixtures. We work closely with your builder to ensure everything is up to code, inspected, and ready for move-in day.

🔌

Add an Outlet — Indoor or Outdoor

Running out of places to plug in? We install new outlets wherever you need them — kitchen counters, home offices, garages, patios, or the exterior of your home. All outdoor outlets are installed weatherproof and GFCI-protected to keep your family safe in any condition.

✨

Chandelier Installation

A chandelier can transform a dining room, entryway, or living space. We handle everything — running the wiring, installing the proper junction box to support the weight, mounting, and connecting all the wiring safely so your new fixture shines beautifully from day one.

💨

Ceiling Fan Installation

Ceiling fans improve comfort and cut energy costs year-round. Whether you're replacing an old fan or installing one in a new location, we take care of all the wiring, proper mounting, and switch installation — including adding a new wall switch or remote if needed.

🏗️

Home Addition Electrical

Expanding your home with a new room, sunroom, garage, or basement finish? We handle all the electrical work for your addition — running new circuits from your panel, installing outlets and lighting, and making sure the new space is fully powered, safe, and up to current code.

🚗

EV Car Charger Installation

Charge your electric vehicle safely and efficiently from home. We install Level 2 EV chargers in your garage or driveway — running a dedicated 240V circuit, mounting the charger unit, and ensuring everything is up to code so you wake up every morning with a full charge.

⚡

Any Other Electrical Work

Don't see what you need listed here? No problem. Our master electricians handle all types of residential and commercial electrical work — panel upgrades, breaker replacements, rewiring, troubleshooting, and more. Contact us with your project and we'll provide a free estimate.
